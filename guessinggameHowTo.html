How To Play

  1. Click 'Play' on the main menu and choose a level from the dropdown menu

  2. There are four modes to choose from: Easy, Medium, Hard, and Insane. You have 25 guesses on easy, 15 on medium, 5 on hard, and only one on Insane.

  3. Your opponent is the me, The computer. I am thinking of a number between 1 and 100, and you have to try to guess it!

  4. Enter a number into the text box and we'll tell you if you're right ot wrong.

  5. If you're right, you get the choice to enter your name to be featured on the leaderboard!

  6. Good luck!
